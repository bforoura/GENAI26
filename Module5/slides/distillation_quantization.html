Knowledge Distillation

Think of this as "Condensed Learning." Instead of just training a small model from scratch, we force a "Student" model (8B) to mimic the exact probability distributions of a "Teacher" model (70B).

  • ● Inherits reasoning patterns from massive foundation models.
  • ● Maintains ~95% intelligence at ~10% of the size.
