1. Normalization (Unit Length)
Vnorm =
V √ (x² + y²)
Scaling vectors to a length of 1 ensures we compare direction, not word count or intensity.
2. The Dot Product
A · B = (xA * xB) + (yA * yB)
In normalized space, this value is the Cosine Similarity.
3. Why use Cosine?
Similarity = cos(θ)
If θ is 0°, similarity is 1.0 (Identical).
If θ is 90°, similarity is 0.0 (Unrelated).
