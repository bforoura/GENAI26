1. Pre-training (The Base Model)

Basically "Autocomplete on Steroids." The model has no concept of a "chat"—it is mathematically driven to generate the next most likely token. It absorbs facts and grammar but won't follow instructions reliably.

2. Supervised Fine-Tuning (SFT)

The model is fed thousands of [Prompt + Expected Output] pairs. This "Supervised" layer breaks the autocomplete cycle and teaches the model how to act as an assistant that responds to queries rather than just extending text.

3. RLHF (Alignment)

Human judges rank various model outputs to "reward" helpfulness and "punish" toxicity or hallucinations. This final polish aligns the assistant's behavior with human values, tone, and safety guardrails.
